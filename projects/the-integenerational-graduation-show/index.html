The Integenerational Graduation Show

In June 2022, Design Academy Eindhoven (formerly Academie voor Industriële Vormgeving Eindhoven) presented a special edition of its annual graduation show in Milan, marking the institution’s 75th anniversary. Titled Intergenerational Graduation Show, the exhibition brought together graduation projects spanning seven decades, highlighting the evolution of the Academy's pedagogical vision as well as the shifting roles and practices of design itself.

The visual identity developed for the exhibition was centered on the theme of celebration. Taking rainbow confetti as a conceptual and visual starting point, the design emphasizes both festivity and continuity. At its core is a modular typographic system composed of five custom-built alphabets. These modular letterforms act as building blocks, adaptable across various formats while maintaining a cohesive visual rhythm.

This system allowed for flexibility in communicating across multiple channels and scales—from exhibition signage and printed matter to digital applications—while reinforcing the commemorative tone of the event. The use of color, rhythm, and typographic play reflects the diversity of perspectives across generations of graduates, and mirrors the Academy’s ongoing role as a space for experimentation and renewal within the design field.
