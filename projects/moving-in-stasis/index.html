Moving In Stasis

Moving in Stasis is a physical and digital exhibition presenting the 2020 graduation projects of sixty Design Academy Eindhoven master’s students. Due to pandemic-related delays, the works had not yet been shown in a public setting. The exhibition took place at Gallery 3 By You, Het Nieuwe Instituut in Rotterdam, from March to April 2021, serving as a retrospective presentation six months after graduation.

The exhibition recontextualized the projects within a new spatial and curatorial framework, acknowledging both their original timelines and the temporal displacement caused by the lockdown. Alongside the physical display, a digital platform was developed to expand the exhibition’s accessibility and archive its contents for long-term engagement.

The platform was coded and designed in collaboration with Cinzia Bongino and served as a parallel environment to the gallery space. It enabled users to browse the projects through a structured interface that supported both thematic navigation and individual project deep-dives. Special attention was given to the design system to ensure clarity, responsiveness, and a neutral framework that could accommodate a wide range of content types.

Rather than simulating the spatial experience of the exhibition, the digital platform was designed as a distinct layer of communication, optimized for reading, interaction, and contextual access. The outcome is a hybrid exhibition that responds to the conditions of its time, offering a structured but flexible framework to revisit and reframe a collection of design research developed under highly constrained circumstances.
