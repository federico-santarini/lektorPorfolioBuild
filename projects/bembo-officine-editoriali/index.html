Bembo Officine Editoriali

Bembo Officina Editoriale (Bembo OE) is the publishing imprint of the IUAV University of Venice Doctoral School. Its publications are dedicated to disseminating research, academic contributions, and doctoral work, with a strong emphasis on open access and scientific rigor. The editorial model is based on collaborative processes that actively involve authors and editors in the development of each publication.

As part of this initiative, a custom automated system was developed to streamline the book production workflow. The process begins with author manuscripts written in Markdown and bibliographic data organized using Zotero. From these inputs, the software generates a structured XML file optimized for Adobe InDesign. This XML is then applied to a predefined template, automating a substantial portion of the typesetting phase.

The system significantly reduces manual effort by enabling a reproducible and scalable publishing method, while preserving typographic and editorial quality. It supports the open-access mission of Bembo OE by increasing production efficiency and making the publication of academic research more accessible and sustainable. The approach combines editorial precision with workflow automation, offering a replicable model for academic publishing contexts.
