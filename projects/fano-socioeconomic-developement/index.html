Fano Socioeconomic Developement

Two short publications produced for the municipality of Fano visualize socio-economic data derived from a research project led by the University of Urbino. The project was developed through close collaboration between a team of designers and sociologists to ensure methodological integrity and clarity in visual communication.

The datasets covered topics such as demographic trends, education levels, and employment rates. The design approach focused on translating the structure and nuance of sociological research into a visual form accessible to both local authorities and the general public. Layouts were developed to highlight comparative readings and trend recognition while maintaining fidelity to the source data.

Visual hierarchy, type systems, and color strategies were defined to support multiple levels of engagement—from quick consultation to extended reading. Rather than simply illustrating data, the publications aim to reflect the socio-political implications of the findings, supporting Fano’s policy-making process by offering a legible and informative tool.
