I am an information designer and graphic designer with a focus on the intersection of design, data, and geography. Currently teaching at Design Academy Eindhoven, I explore how design can communicate complex systems through visual and spatial methods. My work spans a range of projects, from academic publishing systems to public exhibitions, with a particular interest in data visualization, cartography, and software development. I aim to create clear, meaningful design that bridges technical content and accessible communication.
